Fly Car Stunt 4

How to Play Fly Car Stunt 4

Fly Car Stunt 4 is an adrenaline-fueled racing game where you take control of futuristic flying cars and race across sky-high tracks filled with thrilling jumps, loops, and obstacles. Your objective is to reach the finish line while avoiding falling off the track and overcoming every challenge in your path.

The game features single-player and two-player modes, allowing you to race against an opponent or test your skills solo. With a variety of unique cars to choose from, you can unlock upgrades and customize your vehicle for the ultimate flying experience.

Soar through the skies, execute jaw-dropping stunts, and conquer increasingly challenging levels in Fly Car Stunt 4!

Controls Guide:

  • Player 1:
    • W - Accelerate
    • S - Brake/Reverse
    • A - Turn Left
    • D - Turn Right
  • Player 2:
    • ↑ - Accelerate
    • ↓ - Brake/Reverse
    • ← - Turn Left
    • → - Turn Right

Flying Tips:

  • Maintain your speed to clear long jumps and avoid falling.
  • Use precise steering to stay on narrow tracks and avoid obstacles.
  • Focus on timing your movements to perform smooth landings.
  • Upgrade your car's stability and speed for better performance in tougher levels.
