How to Play Brain For Monster Truck

Brain For Monster Truck is a fun and creative puzzle game that combines physics with monster truck racing! Your goal is to guide your monster truck safely to the finish line by drawing paths, ramps, or bridges on the screen. Use your imagination and problem-solving skills to overcome obstacles and ensure your truck collects all the stars along the way.

Each level introduces new challenges, such as gaps, barriers, and tricky terrain, requiring you to plan your drawings carefully. The game features colorful graphics, intuitive controls, and an engaging gameplay experience that's perfect for all ages.

Unleash your creativity, think strategically, and prove you're a mastermind in Brain For Monster Truck!

Controls Guide:

  • Mouse/Touch - Draw lines to create paths or bridges for the truck.
  • ↑ - Accelerate the truck.
  • ↓ - Brake/Reverse the truck.

Drawing Tips:

  • Plan your lines carefully to ensure they support the truck's weight.
  • Use short and precise lines for better control and stability.
  • Adjust the angle of your ramps to help the truck navigate gaps and slopes.
  • Collect stars to maximize your score and unlock new levels.
