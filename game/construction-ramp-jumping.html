Construction Ramp Jumping

How to Play Construction Ramp Jumping

Construction Ramp Jumping is an action-packed driving game where you take control of heavy construction vehicles and perform jaw-dropping stunts! Your mission is to launch vehicles off massive ramps, smash through obstacles, and achieve the longest and most destructive jumps possible.

From bulldozers to cranes and other industrial giants, each vehicle offers a unique experience and challenges. Upgrade your vehicles to boost their power, speed, and jump distance. With realistic physics and explosive visuals, every jump is a thrilling spectacle!

Test your skills across various levels, each featuring different ramps, environments, and destruction opportunities. Can you master the art of construction ramp jumping and set new high scores?

Controls Guide:

  • ↑ - Accelerate
  • ↓ - Brake/Reverse
  • ← - Steer Left
  • → - Steer Right
  • Space - Boost

Jumping Tips:

  • Use the boost at the right moment to maximize your jump distance.
  • Aim for obstacles and targets to earn extra points for destruction.
  • Upgrade your vehicle's engine and suspension for better performance.
  • Experiment with different vehicles to find the best one for each level.
